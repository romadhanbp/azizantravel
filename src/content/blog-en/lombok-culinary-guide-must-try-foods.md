Lombok is not only known for its beautiful beaches but also for its delicious cuisine. Here are the must-try foods when visiting Lombok:

## 1. Ayam Taliang

Spicy grilled chicken marinated in traditional Lombok spices. A true flavor explosion!

## 2. Plecing Kangkung

Blanched water spinach served with spicy tomato and chili sauce. A refreshing side dish.

## 3. Nasi Balap Puyung

A rice dish with shredded chicken, fried noodles, and spicy sambal. Simple but delicious.

## 4. Bebek Betutu

Slow-cooked duck wrapped in banana leaves with rich spices.

## 5. Ikan Bakar

Fresh grilled fish with traditional sambal. Best enjoyed at beachside warungs.

## 6. Sate Rembiga

Beef satay from Rembiga village, known for its sweet and savory marinade.

## 7. Lombok Coffee

Don't miss the local coffee! Lombok produces some of the best coffee in Indonesia.
