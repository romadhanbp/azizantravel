The Gili Islands are a group of three small islands off the northwest coast of Lombok. They are known for their pristine beaches, crystal clear water, and vibrant marine life.

## Getting There

The Gili Islands can be reached by speedboat from Lombok or Bali. The journey from Lombok takes about 30 minutes.

## Things to Do

### Snorkeling and Diving
The Gili Islands offer some of the best snorkeling and diving in Indonesia. You can see sea turtles, colorful fish, and beautiful coral reefs.

### Beach Hopping
Each island has its own character. Gili Trawangan is the most lively, Gili Meno is the quietest, and Gili Air is a perfect balance.

### Sunset Watching
The west-facing beaches offer stunning sunsets. Grab a cocktail and enjoy the view.

### Cycling
No motorized vehicles are allowed on the islands. Rent a bicycle and explore at your own pace.

## Where to Stay

From budget hostels to luxury resorts, there's accommodation for every budget.
