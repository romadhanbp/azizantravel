Lombok is one of the most beautiful islands in Indonesia. Here are the top 10 destinations you must visit:

## 1. Gili Trawangan

The most famous Gili Island with crystal clear water and beautiful coral reefs. Perfect for snorkeling and diving.

## 2. Pink Beach

A unique beach with pink-colored sand caused by red coral fragments. One of only a few pink beaches in the world.

## 3. Mount Rinjani

The second highest volcano in Indonesia. Trekking to the summit is a challenging but rewarding experience.

## 4. Kuta Beach Lombok

Different from Kuta Bali, this beach offers pristine white sand and fewer crowds.

## 5. Tanjung Aan

A stunning beach with turquoise water and unique rock formations.

## 6. Senaru Village

The starting point for Rinjani trekking with beautiful rice terrace views.

## 7. Bukit Merese

A hilltop viewpoint offering panoramic views of the coastline.

## 8. Selong Belanak Beach

A crescent-shaped beach with calm water, perfect for swimming.

## 9. Benang Stokel Waterfall

A beautiful waterfall hidden in the lush green forest.

## 10. Sasak Village

Experience traditional Sasak culture and architecture.
